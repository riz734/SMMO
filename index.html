SMMO Tools



Guild Wars Tool

Enter one or more guild IDs. You will get a list of players in those guilds who are attackable (off safemode and 50%+ health). The list will be ordered by the player's gold on hand (most to least).

PvP Tracker Tool

Enter a guild ID. You will get a list of players from that guild and their PK count. Can be used to keep track of PK count for PvP competitions.

Hit Chance Calculator

Enter your player ID. Then enter one or more player IDs of anyone you want to calculate your hitchance against. You will get info about your chance to hit them in pvp.
